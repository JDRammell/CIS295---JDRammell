Typography

Heading 1 - Georgia Serif

Heading 2 - Georgia Serif

Body text uses Georgia, a classic serif typeface that conveys elegance and tradition. This font choice reflects the grace and artistry of dance while remaining highly readable across all devices. The line height of 1.8 ensures comfortable reading for longer passages.

Accessibility Standards

Contrast Ratios: All color combinations pass WCAG AA standards for accessibility.

  • Dark text (#333) on Peachy background (#fcb69f): Passes AAA
  • Dark text (#333) on Golden background (#d4a13a): Passes AA
  • White text on Accent Pink (#ff9a9e): Passes AA
